## Trend 3: Creator vs Content Debate

*Views: 130,005 | Subs: 924 | Ratio: 141x*

Influencers are initiating a micro‑podcast‑style debate: do creators bring more value than generic content? The conversation is fast‑paced, often framed as a “shorts” argument, and tends to spotlight brand comparisons and ROI metrics.

[![Watch Video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/I2ZZmAYhdME/hqdefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I2ZZmAYhdME)

**How to hop on it:** Host a two‑minute challenge: ask creators to demonstrate your software in one minute and then let them critique a competitor’s content in the next minute. Share the videos in a branded playlist to amplify engagement.

**Best for:** Enterprise SaaS, B2B security platforms, Data‑as‑a‑service, SaaS analytics, SaaS infrastructure
